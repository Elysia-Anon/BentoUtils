About Bento Tools

Welcome to Bento Tools, a privacy-first collection of utilities designed for developers, designers, and creators.

Our Mission

We were tired of searching for simple tools like "JSON Formatter" or "Password Generator" and landing on slow websites filled with pop-up ads and confusing layouts. More importantly, we were concerned about data privacy—why should you upload your private JSON data to a server just to format it?

That's why we built Bento Tools with a core philosophy: 100% Client-Side.

Why Use Us?

  • Privacy: Your data never leaves your browser.
  • Speed: No server latency. Instant results.
  • Design: A clean, dark-mode "Bento Grid" interface that is easy on the eyes.
