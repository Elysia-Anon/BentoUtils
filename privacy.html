Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

At Bento Tools (bentoutils.xyz), accessible from https://bentoutils.xyz, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Bento Tools and how we use it.

1. Client-Side Processing

We take your privacy seriously. All tools on this website (including the Password Generator, JSON Formatter, and Video Player) are built using Client-Side Technology. This means that your data (passwords, code, video files) is processed locally on your device's browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Log Files

Bento Tools follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

3. Cookies and Google AdSense

Like any other website, Bento Tools uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences. We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.

You may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
